# Password Management

This page allows you to change your account password to maintain security.

## How to Change Your Password

1.  **Navigate to Account Settings:** Click on your username in the top-right corner and select "Account" from the dropdown menu.
2.  **Locate the Reset Password Form:** This form is the second main card on the Account page.

## Steps to Reset Your Password

1.  **Current password:**
    -   Enter your existing password into this field. This is required to verify your identity before a password change is authorized.

2.  **New password:**
    -   Enter your desired new password.
    -   **Requirement:** The password must be at least 12 characters long.

3.  **Confirm new password:**
    -   Re-enter your new password to ensure it is correct.
    -   An error message will be displayed if the passwords do not match.

4.  **Update Password:**
    -   Click the **"Update password"** button.
    -   Upon successful submission, your password will be changed.

## Important Security Note

For your security, after you successfully change your password, you will be **immediately logged out** of the application. You will need to log back in using your new password to continue. This ensures that any potentially compromised sessions are terminated.
