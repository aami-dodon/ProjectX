# Account Settings

This page allows you to manage your personal information as it appears across the application.

## How to Update Your Settings

1.  **Navigate to Account Settings:** Click on your username in the top-right corner and select "Account" from the dropdown menu.
2.  **Locate the Account Settings Form:** The form is the first main card on the Account page.

## Fields

You can update the following information:

-   **Full Name:**
    -   **Purpose:** This is the name that will be displayed to other users in the application.
    -   **How to Change:** Click into the "Full name" text field and type your desired name.

-   **Email:**
    -   **Purpose:** This is the email address used for logging in, receiving notifications, and for account recovery.
    -   **How to Change:** Click into the "Email" text field and enter your new email address. This field is required and must be a valid email format.

-   **Avatar:**
    -   **Purpose:** This is your profile picture.
    -   **How to Change:**
        1.  Click the "Choose File" or "Browse" button in the Avatar section.
        2.  Select an image file from your computer.
        3.  For best results, use a square image that is at least 240x240 pixels.
        4.  A preview of your new avatar will be displayed.

3.  **Save Your Changes:**
    -   Once you have made your desired changes, click the **"Save changes"** button at the bottom-right of the card.
    -   You will see a confirmation message indicating that your settings have been saved successfully.
